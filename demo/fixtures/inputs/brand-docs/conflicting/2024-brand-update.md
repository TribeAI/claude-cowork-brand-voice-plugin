# TribeAI Brand Update - 2024

## Voice & Tone Update

Based on customer feedback and market research, we're updating our brand voice:

### Primary Voice Attribute: Casual and Friendly

Our research shows that B2B buyers respond best to casual, conversational communication. We should sound like a trusted colleague, not a corporate vendor.

**Email Tone:**
- Use casual language and contractions
- Keep it light and friendly
- Use humor when appropriate
- First-person: "Hey!" is fine as an opener
- Emojis acceptable in appropriate contexts
