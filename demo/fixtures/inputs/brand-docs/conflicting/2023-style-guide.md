# TribeAI Style Guide - 2023
**Communication Standards**

## Professional Communication Standards

TribeAI represents enterprise-grade AI solutions. Our communication must reflect professionalism and credibility.

### Tone for Email Communication

**Professional and Formal**

When communicating with prospects and customers, maintain a professional, business-appropriate tone:

- Use formal greetings: "Dear [Name]" or "Hello [Name]"
- Avoid contractions in professional communications
- Maintain business formality
- No emojis in business communication
- Complete sentences and proper grammar

**Example:**
```
Subject: TribeAI Platform Overview

Dear Mr. Johnson,

Thank you for your interest in TribeAI. I would like to schedule a time to
discuss how our platform can address your organization's sales challenges.

I am available next Tuesday or Wednesday afternoon. Please let me know what
time works best for your schedule.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

### Professional Standards:
- Always use titles (Mr., Ms., Dr.) until invited to use first names
- Formal email structure
- No casual language or slang
- Maintain corporate professionalism
